    • National Technical and Vocational Training Sponsored by Germany Government

    Funded and Hosted by the German Government to Empower Nigerian Youths and Teenagers with Practical Skills and Versatility

    Note: Application Closes on the 30th of March 2025

    President Bola Tinubu has reiterated the administration’s dedication to building a more robust and fruitful relationship between Germany and Nigeria. Tinubu underlined the value of establishing strong ties that benefit both countries while speaking at a bilateral meeting with German President Frank-Walter Steinmeier on Wednesday at the State House in Abuja.

    The Germany Government has now decided to work with different traning and Vocational centers in Nigeria to empower Nigeria Youths and Teenagers with Tech Skills and Vocational Skills for free.

    Benefits of the Program

  • Skill Acquisition: Top-notch vocational and technical training in various fields.
  • Stipend Support: Monthly stipend of #100,000 the 12-month training and internship period.
  • Internship Opportunities: Practical experience through internships after training.
  • Job Placement: Collaboration with the Organized Private Sector (OPS) and government for job placements.
    • Entrepreneurship Support: Mentorship, Resourses and Equipment for successful Graduates.

    Eligibility

  • Nigerian citizens aged 18 to 45.
  • Minimum secondary school certificate SSCE or equivalent (Optional).
  • Commitment to the 6-month program. (Flexible for working selected applicant)
  • Priority for unemployed youths and young individuals.
  • Must have a means of Identification (NIN, Drivers Liences, Voters card or International passport)

    • Application Process

  • Online Application
  • Screening Process: Assessment and verification of Applicant ID.
  • Commitment to the 6-month program. (Flexible for working selected applicant)
  • Notification: Email or SMS notifications for successful applicants.
  • Orientation: Orientation at Training centers before program commencement.
